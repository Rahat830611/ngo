Woman Help Service


The main task of this organization is to work for human rights. This group has been carrying the responsibility of restoring the rights of the disenfranchised since on the side of all professions or the side of the disenfranchised. To provide legal advice or legal aid to any class of people (help-helpless, women-children, rich-poor) free of cost. We provide legal assistance through the courts for those who apply for legal assistance through us. Our wise legal practitioners are always ready to provide you with legal advice and assistance.

1. You can call inbox or get free legal advice. The address or identity of the caller / complainant is kept confidential; 2. The admins consider resolving the issue through discussion through group chat; The case recorder records the case and takes the next step and sends the case to the court. Many fraudsters ask for money in the name of this group. If this happens, I will report it to the admin. Admins never accept money; 04. Attempts are made to resolve the issue through ADR. And at the same time the following actions are followed.

# Assist the administration on the basis of communication with the administration and provide legal advice and assistance to the victims;

# Take steps to bring corrupt people and those who plunder state resources under the law; Providing legal advice and assistance to fraudulent expatriates;

# In order to create public awareness, meetings, seminars, discussion meetings, dramas etc. should be presented to the people;

# Provide legal advice and assistance to people convicted without guilt; # Work to prevent child labor;

# Provide legal advice and assistance to victims of child abuse, acid terrorism, child trafficking, child marriage and dowry prevention;

# To ensure that no one is subjected to torture or cruel, inhuman or degrading treatment or punishment; ;

# Everyone has the right to an adequate standard of living for the health and well-being of himself and his family This right includes access to food, clothing, shelter, medical care and other necessary social services and security in case of unemployment, illness, disability, widowhood, old age or other unavoidable circumstances. Everyone has the right to special care and assistance during maternity and childhood.

# All children will enjoy the same social security, whether they are born out of wedlock or out of wedlock. Spreading this information among the common people.
