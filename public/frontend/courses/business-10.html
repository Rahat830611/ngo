MAYNA WELLFARE FOUNDATION  Is a Non-Government, voluntary, social welfare Organization has emerged from which is the initiatives of some like-minded dedicated social worker of our locality of under the district of Tangail. Since inception it has been working with a view to facilitate activities though social groups members learn to manage their affairs and respond to the unique and changing needs of their community rather than following a rigid model has an Executive Committee. The Executive Committee formed by the General Committee the 7 Members Executive Committee. The EC is responsible for overall management of the Organization. The Chairman is the Chief Executive of the Organization working of Districts has been providing for Group formation income generating and Employment Creation Health and family planning Fisheries Development program, Tree Plantation, Sanitary latrine program, Community based tube well installation, Nutrition and Nutrition food popularization campaign, Legal aid, Self employment for poultry, Human Resource Development Training, Mother and Child Care and women development. A well set up of staff, which has a lot of experiences to handle the respective project activities. All the staff is also well experienced to perform their duties and responsibilities perfectly.
